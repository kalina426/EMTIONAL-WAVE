Summer Rainstorms

In my scent memory, the summer rainy season always comes in a hurry and rush, and the smell of the rainy season's humidity gives me a very bland and gentle feeling.and as the rain subsides, its delicate trace lingers like a soft caress on my skin. The damp earth, mingled with a whisper of wildflowers and distant memories, seems to invite me into a quiet reverie. In those fleeting moments, the world feels both vast and intimate—a secret shared between nature and my soul. The rush of the downpour washes away the mundane, leaving behind a serene canvas where each droplet narrates tales of summers past, fleeting joys, and promises of renewal.
